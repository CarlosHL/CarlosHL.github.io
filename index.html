About Me

My name is Carlos Herrero and I am an Spanish double degree student of Computer Science and Business Management from the University Charles III of Madrid.

Currently I am studying my senior year in Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder, as a part of an exchange program. Here, I am trying to get the best of my international program while getting involved in Boulder's community. With that intention I joined some of the university clubs and associations, as BFIS (Boulder Friends of International Students), Mobile Makers club focused in the development of mobile Apps, and HackCU, the organization in charge of Colorado's biggest student hackathon. As a member of HackCU, I work with the sponsorship team, helping fundraising money and getting mentors to help the hackers during the hackathon.

My main area of interest in Computer Science are Algorithms althought I really enjoy working in front-end since it allows me to start new projects by myself.

Regarding to my Business Management studies, I am interested mainly in Marketing and Markets Research, specially in all concerning the technologic sector.

I am currently looking for internship opportunities in Europe or the United States to develop my faculties and exploit my international skills.
